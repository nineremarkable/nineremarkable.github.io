Thai Massage:

"Thai massage" or "Thai yoga massage" is an ancient healing system combining acupressure, Indian Ayurvedic principles, and assisted yoga postures. [citation needed] In the Thai language it is usually called nuat phaen thai (Thai: นวดแผนไทย; lit. "Thai-style massage") or nuat phaen boran (Thai: นวดแผนโบราณ, IPA: [nûət pʰɛ̌ːn boːraːn]; lit. "ancient-style massage"), though its formal name is nuat thai (Thai: นวดไทย, lit. Thai massage) according to the Traditional Thai Medical Professions Act, BE 2556 (2013).The Thai Ministry of Health's Department for Development of Thai Traditional and Alternative Medicine regulates Thai traditional massage venues and practitioners. As of 2016 the department says 913 traditional clinics have registered nationwide in Thailand.

Top Medical Service:

Thailand's medical industry is known as the "Asian Medical Center". There are currently 53 hospitals in Thailand that have received the JCI certification from international authoritative organizations. Doctors usually received training in European and American countries. However, the charges are only 1/4 compare to Europe and the United States. At the same time, medical services here in Thailand is foreigner friendly. A foreign resident could easily get medical integrated services which including visa signing, traveling, inspecting, surgery, convalescence and travel customization. Seven Stars International Resort’s residents could enjoy exclusive five-star international medical services at Bangkok Pattaya Hospital conveniently.

Insurance System：

Nowadays, Thailand has become the world's largest tourism medical destination. More than 2 million people visit Thailand every year for medical services. Here, many private hospitals have a five-star hotel-like medical environment and services: magnificent lighting, colorful fountains, gorgeous halls, and the air is full of Starbucks coffee smells. Outside the window, there are silver sands and blue water stretch far away. Many hospitals collaborating with Seven Stars International Resort offer a variety of medical insurance options to erase your worries: Thailand's medical insurance is mostly global claims, so even if you return home, you could still claim compensation. Its medical insurance mainly includes medical expenses caused by accidents, hospital-generated wards, treatment costs, laboratory tests, minor operations and other cost claims. After presenting your insurance card directly when you seek medical treatment could simply be done at the hospital. Hospitals will charge the bill directly to your insurance companies.
