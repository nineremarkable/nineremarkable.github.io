Welcome to Seven Stars International Resort and Palm Spring Villas

Seven Stars International Resort and Palm Spring Villas are located in Pattaya, a famous tourist attraction in Thailand. Pattaya is known for beautiful weather and perfect location as for vacation destination, real estate investment, tourism, and retirement relaxation in Southeast Asia. It gains the reputation of "Oriental Hawaii" and has become synonymous with "Beach Vacation Paradise." Pattaya is famous for its sunshine, beach and seafood. Its beautiful seascape, novel paradise, the most prestigious shemale performances, and colorful nightlife attracts tourists from all over the world. Seven Stars International Resort is located close to Pattaya's Jomtien Beach. It is located at the perfect location which between the mountains and the ocean. Jomtien Beach is in the middle of Pattaya. There are many dining and entertainment facilities along the Jomtien Beach walking distinct. Come and experience a exotic style in Pattaya!
